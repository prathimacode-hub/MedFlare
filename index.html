Services

Here are the services offered to our customers.

Vaccination Centers Notification


We aim to provide time to time update about the recent number of COVID-19 vaccination doses available for all age groups from children to adults. Every variety of doses and slot bookings are smoothly bestowed to our customers in this section.

Oxygen Cylinders Supply


Oxygen cylinders with oxygen masks are supplied to the needy patients in the wink of an eye in our customer services. We value the worth of each oxygen cylinder in recuperating the excruciating condition of a helpless patient.

Virtual Health Checkup


In this pandemic times, it's exigent for every individual to stay in home as far as possible. In order to facilitate the process of health checkup from home, we bring forward this initiative of virtual health checkup in our customer care sector.

Bed Availability Notification


With the plethora of patients being reported COVID-19 positive every hour, we tried to ease the process of bed availability by including the feature of bed availability notification in our customer services. Now lives won't be lost just due to the paucity of information about bed availabilities in the hospitals.

Blood donors availability


We tried to consolidate the nexus between the blood donors and the patients by incorporating the feature of blood donors availability in our services sector. This would alarm the doctors about the availability of a specific blood group ,and would also save the time wastage in the process of obtaining the suitable blood donor.

Doctors Appointment


Time is precious for all, so coming to the doctor's house for making an appointment has been curtailed to making a virtual appointment with the doctor through our customer services.Our authorities will look after the virtual automated process of fixing the doctor-patient meeting in a collaborative way.
